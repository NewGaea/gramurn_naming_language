laa
===

**laa** _verb root_

1. to be aware, 
2. to have a soul

`#2000`

### Other Forms ###

> **khara** _desc_ hunting, of the hunt

> **kharen** _tool element_ spearhead, arrowhead

> **kharm** _tool of_ spear, arrow, javelin

> **laan** _noun_ soul, spirit

> **kharon** _doer_ hunter
